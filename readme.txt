\outl0\strokewidth0 \strokec2 My term project is a two-player game called ROTA. The ROTA board is a wheel with 8 circles surrounding a circle in the middle. There are spokes going from the center circle to all the other circles, and the pieces of the ROTA game can move along these spokes. The player can place three pieces on the board and once they have all three pieces on the board, they play by moving the pieces along the spokes. The first player to get all three pieces in a row wins. I\'92m building an AI for this game using the minimax algorithm. I also implement a multi-player mode using sockets.
